Ratings Key

5. Outstanding Performance - Exemplary accomplishments throughout the rating period and/or represented by unique and unusual accomplishments and result in specifically identifiable or quantifiable benefit to System Administration or the nstitutions.

4. Very Good Performance - Exceeded the expectations and requirements of the assigned position and regularly produces expected accomplishments in all areas of responsibility.

3. Good Performance - Meets job expectations and requirements and may occasionally exceed performance objectives.

2. Needs Improvement - May meet some of the job expectations but does not fully meet the remainder.

1. Unsatisfactory Performance - Fails to meet defined expectations and minimum job requirements.




Exhibit job-relevant knowledge and skill needed to perform the duties and requirements of the position.



Understands the importance of quality service and delivers quality service.


Demonstrates effective written and oral communication skills.


Analyzes and prioritizes situations to identify and solve problems.


See things to be done and then take appropriate action without being so directed and understands the value of innovation and of quality improvement.


Applies skills and knowledge to achieve department and organizational goals.
