ISEER Recommendations Competencies




Behavioural Anchors

Ratings Key

5. Outstanding Performance - Exemplary accomplishments throughout the rating period and/or represented by unique and unusual accomplishments and result in specifically identifiable or quantifiable benefit to System Administration or the institutions.

4. Very Good Performance - Exceeded the expectations and requirements of the assigned position and regularly produces expected accomplishments in all areas of responsibility.

3. Good Performance - Meets job expectations and requirements and may occasionally exceed performance objectives.

2. Needs Improvement - May meet some of the job expectations but does not fully meet the remainder.

1. Unsatisfactory Performance - Fails to meet defined expectations and minimum job requirements.




Report to work on a timely basis and stay on the job.


Being where he/she should be doing what he/she is supposed to do.


Adjusting to change without chaos and initiate or recommend beneficial changes in work procedures.


Includes and welcomes others and works to understand the perspectives of others.


Builds productive working relationship.


Working with people without conflict.
